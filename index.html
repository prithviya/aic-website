School Innovation Challenge 2022

About

To highlight the talents of young entrepreneurs around the Nation, Rathinam Public School, in collaboration with AIC RAISE, has initiated a CEO JR. The program’s goal is to bring the best minds across the nation to develop solutions for the most pressing environmental problems and, to create awareness among young minds.

The Initiative’s focus is to educate school students about environmental issues and in order to provide their ideas as remedies. We aim to create a potential business CEO’s, they will gather, discuss and address those problems, by provide solutions through innovative challenge.

On June 4, 2022, the CEO JR. launched by the Honourable Minister of Electricity Mr. Senthil Balaji at the Rathinam Group of Institutions.

Road Map

  • Outreach
    Outreach and reaching the schools and young skills.
  • Registration
    Students ideas registration/Submission.
  • Idea submission/ Selection
    Students submitting their ideas as teams for problem categories and shortlisting ideas to provide prototype support.
  • Bootcamp
    Selecting students’ ideas and providing prototype support.
  • Grand Finale
    Presenting the best performed top ideas in Grand Finale.

Eligibility Criteria & Impact

  1. Step One
    Any Indian school going student of Class VIII to XII
  2. Step Two
    Below 18 years of age on 1st June, 2022.
  3. Step Three
    The concept of the innovation should have been proved through a model, a prototype or an experimental data.
  4. Step Four
    Prizes and Cash award prizes.
